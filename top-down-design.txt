Making A Sandwich

1.1 Get to and operate Refrigerator
  1.1.1 Walk to Refrigerator
    1.1.1.1 Pick up left leg
    1.1.1.2 Step forward
    1.1.1.3 Pick up right leg
    1.1.1.4 Step forward
    1.1.1.5 continue process until destination reached.
  1.1.2 Open Refrigerator
    1.1.2.1 Open Hand
    1.1.2.2 Reach out with right hand
    1.1.2.3 Locate Handle
    1.1.2.4 Put open hand on Handle
    1.1.2.5 Close hand around handle
    1.1.2.6 Pull open Refrigerator door
1.2 Gather ingredients
    1.2.1 Locate Ham
    1.2.2 Grab Ham
    1.2.3 Locate Cheese
    1.2.4 Grab Cheese
    1.2.5 Locate Mayo
    1.2.6 Grab Mayo
    1.2.7 Locate Bread
    1.2.8 Grab Bread
    1.2.9 Locate Counter space
    1.2.10 Walk to counter space
     1.2.10.1 Pick up left leg
     1.2.10.2 Step forward
     1.2.10.3 Pick up right leg
     1.2.10.4 Step forward
     1.2.10.5 continue process until destination reached.
    1.2.11 Place ingredients on counter top
1.3 Construct Sandwich
  1.3.1 Open Bread
  1.3.2 Pull out two pieces of Bread
    1.3.2.1 Reach in to bread bag
    1.3.2.2 Open hand
    1.3.2.3 Grab first piece
    1.3.2.4 Place on Counter
    1.3.2.5 Reach into bread bag
    1.3.2.6 Open hand
    1.3.2.7 Grab second piece of Bread
    1.3.2.8 place on Counter
  1.3.3 Spread Mayo on Bread
    1.3.3.1 Locate knife
    1.3.3.2 Pick up knife
    1.3.3.3 Open Mayo
    1.3.3.4 Put knife in mayo jar and get some mayo on knife
    1.3.3.5 Spread mayo on Bread
  1.3.4 Place Ham on sandwich
    1.3.4.1 open ham container
    1.3.4.2 grab Ham
    1.3.4.3 place ham on bread/Mayo
  1.3.5 Place cheese on sandwich
    1.3.5.1 open cheese container
    1.3.4.2 grab Cheese
    1.3.4.3 place cheese on bread/mayo/Ham
  1.3.6 Close sandwich
    1.3.6.1 pick up one piece of Bread
    1.3.6.2 place bread on other half meat side up
  1.4 Eat sandwich
